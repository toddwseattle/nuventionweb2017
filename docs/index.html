The NUvention Web Teaching team is focused on helping students gain the experience and be successful in a two quarter course at Northwestern University where they build a web or digital media business. The course is taught at the http://www.farley.northwestern.edu

The course has been offered since 2010

More Information on the Class

Goals

  • Learn to work in a multidicsciplinary team to develop a software business
  • Understand how to apply Lean Startup techniques to identify and develop a market opportunity
  • Learn how to work in a team to develop Real Software that meets a users need
  • How to effectively assess user needs and refine a product to meet them
  • How to launch a product and acquire customers
